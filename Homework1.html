Visualisation of Datasets¶

Customer Price Index Histogram¶

In [70]:
ggplot(data=cpi, aes(x=month(Tarih), y=cpival))+geom_bar(stat='identity')+
    facet_wrap(~year(Tarih))+
    theme(legend.position = "none",
         axis.ticks.x=element_blank(),
         axis.text.x= element_blank())+
    xlab("Years") + ylab("CPI")+ ggtitle("CPI in Turkey from 2017 to 2021")

A gradual increase can be seen throughout the span of 5 years. However, the rate of increase saw a boost in the last 2 years, with the last datapoint being the current peak (likely to be toppled soon).

This can be said to be a significant reason that drove the boost in spite of the rise in prices and fall in purchasing power of the general public.
